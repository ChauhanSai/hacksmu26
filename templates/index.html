Audio-Only Elephant Cleaner

Upload one recording and get an auto-detected rumble-only result with before and after spectrograms.

The cleaner now generates its own spectrogram from the audio, detects likely elephant-rumble regions from low-frequency and harmonic structure, suppresses stationary noise and tonal lines, and then plots the cleaned result so you can compare what was removed.

Flow

  1. Upload the noisy WAV.
  2. The app builds the before spectrogram automatically.
  3. It detects likely rumble windows from the recording itself.
  4. It cleans and extracts the rumble-only audio.
  5. You compare the before and after spectrograms side by side.

Input

Run the cleaner

The app will auto-generate the before spectrogram, detect rumble regions, clean the signal in the elephant band, and then render the after spectrogram.
