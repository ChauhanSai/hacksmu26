BRIEFING · 001

A behavioral correlation engine for elephant vocalizations.

Pachyderm Intelligence is a computational bioacoustics terminal that ingests low-frequency elephant calls, extracts a twenty-dimensional acoustic signature from each one, and maps those signatures onto observed behavioral context — feeding, greeting, distress, mating, coalition-building — with calibrated confidence scores.

It is not a translator. It is a signal-intelligence platform for researchers and conservationists: cluster the repertoire, find the context-specific "words," identify individuals by voice, and surface the calls that deviate from baseline.
